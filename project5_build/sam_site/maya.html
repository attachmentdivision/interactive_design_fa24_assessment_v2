Maya Tihtiyas Attean

is a Wabanaki artist based in Portland, Maine. Raised on the Penobscot Nation Reservation (Alnabe Menahan), her art process reflects her ancestry, resilience, and culture. Her transition from reservation life to city living informs her perspective, merging techniques from multiple cultures. Maya earned her BFA with honors in Photography from Maine College of Art & Design in 2023 and is a 2024 BIPOC artist studio awardee at SPACE Gallery in Portland, Maine.
